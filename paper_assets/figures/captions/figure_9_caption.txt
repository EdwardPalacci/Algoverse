Figure 9. Dataset-level accuracy and expected calibration error (ECE) by model family, pooled across all three prompt conditions. AR pools three autoregressive models and DLM pools four diffusion language models. Accuracy uses all judged generations; ECE uses generations with parsed confidence. Points are family-level estimates; horizontal bars are 95% bootstrap standard-error intervals over question identifiers, preserving all models, prompt conditions, and repeated generations associated with each resampled question. Higher accuracy and lower ECE are better.

Figure 3. Reliability diagrams by prompt condition and model family. Rows show cautious, neutral, and overconfident prompts; columns show autoregressive language models (AR) and diffusion language models (DLMs). Points report mean verbalized confidence and empirical accuracy in each non-empty confidence bin. Points are not connected, and bins with at most five observations are labeled directly; all bin counts are available in the companion CSV. The dashed diagonal denotes perfect calibration.

Figure 10. Neutral-prompt reliability diagram comparing autoregressive language models (AR), pooled across three models, with diffusion language models (DLMs), pooled across four models. Points show mean verbalized confidence and empirical accuracy within 10 equal-width confidence bins; rows without parsed confidence are omitted. Vertical bars show Wilson 95% confidence intervals for empirical bin accuracy; sparse bins with fewer than 20 observations are labeled. The dashed diagonal denotes perfect calibration.

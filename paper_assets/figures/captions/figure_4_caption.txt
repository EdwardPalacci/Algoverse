Figure 4. AR/DLM reliability diagram using 10 equal-width bins of verbalized confidence normalized to [0, 1]. Empty bins are omitted. The dashed diagonal denotes perfect calibration, where empirical accuracy equals mean confidence. Curves compare autoregressive language models (AR) and diffusion language models (DLMs) using the saved LLM-as-judge correctness labels. Bin counts are saved in the companion CSV because sparse bins can produce visually extreme points.
